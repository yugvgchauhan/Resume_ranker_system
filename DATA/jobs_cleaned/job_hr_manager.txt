Job Title Human Resources HR Manager
Responsibilities
- Manage recruitment, onboarding, and employee engagement activities.
- Develop HR policies and ensure compliance with labor laws.
- Oversee performance management, promotions, and employee retention programs.
- Conduct training sessions and handle employee grievances.
- Collaborate with management to align HR strategy with business goals.
Requirements
- Degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.
- Proven experience in HR operations and people management.
- Strong communication, interpersonal, and leadership skills.
- Knowledge of labor laws, payroll systems, and compliance requirements.
- Ability to work with confidential information responsibly.
