Job Title Cybersecurity Specialist
Responsibilities
- Monitor and secure IT systems against cyber threats.
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
- Implement firewalls, intrusion detection, and encryption protocols.
- Investigate security breaches and provide incident reports.
- Stay updated with the latest cybersecurity trends and regulations.
Requirements
- Knowledge of networking protocols and operating systems.
- Hands-on experience with SIEM tools and firewalls.
- Familiarity with ethical hacking and penetration testing tools.
- Strong problem-solving and risk assessment skills.
- Security certifications CEH, CISSP are a plus.
