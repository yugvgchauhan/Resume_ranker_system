Job Title: UI/UX Designer

Responsibilities:
- Design user-friendly interfaces and improve user experience.
- Conduct user research, usability testing, and feedback sessions.
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design systems.
- Work closely with developers to ensure design consistency.
- Stay updated with design trends and best practices.

Requirements:
- Proficiency in design tools (Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch).
- Strong portfolio showcasing UI/UX projects.
- Understanding of design principles, typography, and color theory.
- Knowledge of accessibility standards.
- Strong communication and collaboration skills.
