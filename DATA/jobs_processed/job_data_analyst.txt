Job Title: Data Analyst

Responsibilities:
- Gather, clean, and analyze structured and unstructured datasets.
- Generate dashboards and reports to support business decisions.
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and identify trends.
- Work closely with stakeholders to define KPIs and reporting needs.
- Use visualization tools to communicate insights effectively.

Requirements:
- Strong skills in SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI).
- Experience with Python/R for data manipulation.
- Knowledge of statistics and hypothesis testing.
- Ability to turn business requirements into actionable analysis.
- Good communication and presentation skills.
